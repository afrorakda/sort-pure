# #99 Sort Pure

**Sort Pure** is a minimalist, high-performance list sorter designed for those who need a clean, "human-first" alphabetical order without the hassle of complex settings. It ignores case sensitivity and handles numbers naturally, providing the exact order you expect.

## 1 Tool / 1 Action
- **One Action**: Sorts any text list into a clean, case-insensitive, and naturally ordered sequence (A to Z) with a single tap.

## Features
- **Case-Insensitive**: Mix "Apple" and "apple" without splitting them up.
- **Natural Numeric Sort**: Orders numbers like a human (1, 2, 10) instead of a machine (1, 10, 2).
- **Symbol Resilient**: Keeps your words grouped together even if they contain brackets, hyphens, or punctuation.
- **Instant Copy**: Automatically copies the sorted result to your clipboard the moment you hit the button.
- **Privacy First**: No login, no tracking, no ads. Browser-only processing.

## How to Use
1. **Paste**: Drop your messy list into the viewer.
2. **Sort**: Tap the **SORT (A→Z)** button.
3. **Done**: Your list is instantly sorted and copied. The button will flash "COPIED!" to confirm.

## Design System
- **Background**: #fff (White)
- **Main Color**: #333 (Dark Gray)
- **Accent**: #eee / #fafafa
- **Radius**: 15px (Strict Protocol)

## Privacy Policy
- No login required.
- No tracking or data collection.
- No ads.
- All processing happens locally in your browser.
